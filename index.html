Auditions

We have three audition paths: Recruit Program, Revive Program and Indies Program!

The Recruit program is simply joining as you are and with what assets you have! No need to concern yourself with a redebut or taking on an entirely different persona! Recruits are often scouted Talents that will be approached by members of our team. The Revive program is a pay to enter program. You would receive a full redesign: model, streaming assets, banner, pfp, overlays, and more, with the full support of our Artist team! Lastly, our Indie Program is a support program. Talents under the open Indie Program will not receive the same program access as Recruits and Revives, but they will receive marketing support and managment. All programs still require an initial audition and acceptance into our group!

About Apeiron

Apeiron Alive is a Vtuber Management and Collective that works towards assiting Indie Vtubers with their streaming dreams! Working with us, our Alivers gain experience in the field of Vtubing and earn easier access to assets for thier own personal growth. Aside from our main priorities of marketing, we also help our Alivers produce covers, events, and merchandise!

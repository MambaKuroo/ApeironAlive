About Apeiron

Apeiron Alive is a Vtuber Agency group dedicated to helping smaller Indie VTubers grow and meet their goals! This is a group run by AL-YX, our Android, and VTubers, to create a comfortable and safe Vtuber Corporation with no secrets and no disputes. Which ever Star Path you take with us, you have our infinite support.

Star Paths

Auditions

We have two audition paths: Recruit Program and Revive Program!

The Recruit program is simply joining as you are and with what assets you have! No need to concern yourself with a redebut or taking on an entirely different persona! The Revive program is a pay to enter program. You would receive a full redesign: model, streaming assets, banner, pfp, overlays, and more, with the full support of our Artist team! Both programs still require an initial audition and acceptance into our group!

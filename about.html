About Apeiron

Meet the Creators

Apeiron Alive was an idea in the works since February 2024. The two creators behind it all are our very own Mamba Kuroo and Saku Akuma. Mamba sprung the idea a while back and the both of them started up a small server based off the idea that would operate out of public view. But after sometime with their iron friendship, they decided it should be bigger and brighter. Being Indie Creators themselves, they understood how hard it can be, especially for those who have large goals in the Vtubing world. After a lot of thought and much research, the idea started to shine. Members of Volume One were gathered and even offered to help with launch and became needed gears to the process. Vtubers helping Vtubers, that's the goal, and that's who we are. We hope you join us on our adventure through the infinite...
